About Me

Hello! My name is Rona May Balangat, a 4th-year college student currently pursuing a Bachelor of Science in Information Technology. As part of my academic journey, I am undertaking my On-the-Job Training (OJT) at Iraya Energies Inc. This internship is a valuable opportunity for me to apply my knowledge, enhance my technical skills, and gain hands-on experience in a professional setting.

About Iraya Energies Inc.

Iraya Energies Inc. is a data-driven company that specializes in integrating engineering, geoscience expertise, and big data analytics to revolutionize the energy industry. Established in 2016, the company operates in Kuala Lumpur, Stavanger, and Houston, offering cutting-edge solutions for energy optimization and resource management.
At Iraya, a team of data scientists, engineers, and geoscientists works with advanced technologies, including Python programming, database-building, machine learning algorithms, and web visualization tools. Their goal is to process and analyze massive amounts of historical and real-time data, enabling smarter and more efficient decision-making in the energy sector.

Interning at Iraya Energies provides a hands-on experience in modern technological solutions, fostering skills in data analysis, web development, and system design while contributing to sustainable and innovative energy projects.
