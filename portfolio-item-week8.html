A Week of WordPress Development: Troubleshooting and Moving Forward

Week 8: Troubleshooting the Demo Page and iframe Issues (March 24 - 28, 2025)

This week was full of technical challenges, problem-solving, and some breakthroughs, as we continued working on the Iraya Energies demo page using WordPress and Elementor.

We started the week with the completion of the demo page, where we were able to finalize the design with a few minor adjustments. However, the real challenge came when we tried to embed the demo's iframe. Our main issue was that we needed the iframe to be dynamic, as it had to reflect different products depending on the demo page. This became our primary focus for the rest of the week.

By mid-week, we were working on finding a way to use a single iframe for both products. Without a database, this task proved to be more difficult than we initially expected. Despite the obstacles, we kept pushing forward, experimenting with different approaches to make the iframe work seamlessly for both product demos.
